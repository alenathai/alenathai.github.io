About Us

Welcome to Morning Glory, your home away from home for authentic Vietnamese cuisine in the heart of San Luis Obispo! Situated in this vibrant college town, Morning Glory stands as one of the rare beacons of true Vietnamese flavors amidst a sea of diverse culinary offerings.

Here, we understand the longing for the taste of home, especially when home feels miles away. That's why we've made it our mission to bring the comforting and familiar taste of authentic Vietnamese dishes to this vibrant community. With each bite, we aim to transport you to the bustling streets of Saigon or the serene countryside of Hanoi, offering a culinary journey that's as enriching as it is delicious.

But Morning Glory is more than just a restaurant – it's a gathering place where friends and family come together to share laughter, stories, and of course, exceptional food. Our warm and inviting atmosphere invites guests to linger over their meals, creating memories that linger long after the last bite is savored.

Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur of Vietnamese cuisine or embarking on your first culinary adventure, we invite you to join us at Morning Glory. Come experience the taste of home in a place that may be far from it – because no matter where you are, our flavors will always remind you of the comfort and warmth of home.
